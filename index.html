This tool provides an estimate for informational purposes only and is not a diagnosis. Reference ranges follow American Diabetes Association guidelines: normal below 5.7%, prediabetes 5.7%–6.4%, diabetes 6.5% or above. Always confirm results and interpretation with a doctor or lab report.

How the conversion works

eAG is derived from HbA1c using the linear formula published from the ADAG study, which correlated A1C lab values against continuous glucose monitoring data over three months.

eAG (mg/dL) = 28.7 × A1C − 46.7 eAG (mmol/L) = 1.59 × A1C − 2.59

HbA1c reflects average blood glucose over roughly the previous 2–3 months, weighted more heavily toward the most recent 30 days.
